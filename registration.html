Registration

  • Registration

Once papers have been accepted, authors need to register by paying the registration fee. The payment details are given below:

Delegate Category Registration Fees
Indian students INR 2000
Research Scholars from India INR 2500
Faculty members from India INR 3000
Overseas Scholars USD 120

Instructions for Author :
The registration Fee includes conference kit, lunch, tea/coffee. It does not include journal publication charges. Payment can be made through a Demand Draft drawn in favour of "The Principal, Stella Maris College" payable at "Chennai". Please mention name/s of the participants along with a contact number on the reverse of the Demand Draft. In the case of collaborative papers, all participants will need to register individually.
Accepted research papers will be published in the conference proceedings and will be sent to UGC CARE listed or Scopus indexed journals to be considered for publications. Author will be informed via mail. Extra Fees for publication in Scopus Indexed Journal needs to be paid separately.
